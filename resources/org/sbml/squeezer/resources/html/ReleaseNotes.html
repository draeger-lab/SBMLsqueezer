Release Notes of SBMLsqueezer version 1.4

Download of SBMLsquezeezer

You can always download the latest version of SBMLsqueezer at http://www.ra.cs.uni-tuebingen.de/software/SBMLsqueezer/downloads.

New Features

  • Simulator for SBML
  • Based on official JSBML
  • New rate law for crowded reaction spaces

Bug Fixes

The units of the association and dissociation constant in generalized mass action kinetics could not be derived correctly if a catalyst interfered with the reaction, because the units of the catalyst were not taken into account.

The units of the half saturation constant in the (generalized) Hill equation were not correctly derived.

In GUI, species tag was not visible for SimpleSpeciesReferences due to an incorrect positioning of the GUI element.
